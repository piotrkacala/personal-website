# Product

Personal website for Piotr Kacała — the third career surface (after CV and LinkedIn). A single-page site where the full positioning and worldview live. Visitors are potential employers or consulting clients who have already seen a CV or LinkedIn profile and want to know more.

## Core Principles

- **Two-layer portfolio.** The site works on two levels simultaneously: the content (who Piotr is, what he's built) and the code (how the site was built — public repo, commit history, CLAUDE.md, docs-driven workflow, zero-code methodology). A visitor who only reads the site gets one picture; a visitor who opens the repo gets the full one.
- The site is a controlled space, not a pitch letter. Tone is neutral — works for both employer and consulting reader.
- Output speaks first. The mojeaudyty.pl productivity story is the strongest proof point — it must be prominent and specific, not buried.
- No separate sections for skills, about, or bio. Who you are is demonstrated by what you've built, not listed.
- Every word earns its place. No filler, no marketing language.
- Available in English (primary) and Polish. Polish is Piotr's first language; a Polish version reaches a different part of the audience and signals local context.

## Visitor Flow

Single user type: someone evaluating Piotr as a developer, consultant, or potential hire.

**Flow:** Visitor arrives → reads positioning statement and expansion → reads project narrative → optionally contacts.

**Acceptance criteria:**

- Visitor can understand what Piotr does and why it's unusual within 10 seconds of landing.
- Projects section conveys a deliberate arc, not a random collection.
- mojeaudyty.pl productivity numbers are present, specific, and readable.
- Contact action is clear and requires no explanation.
- Site loads fast and reads correctly on mobile.

## Launch Scope

The following must be present and working at v1:

- Section 1: Positioning — single headline, short expansion (4–6 sentences max)
- Section 2: Projects — Phonetic, Surfaced, mojeaudyty.pl with narrative arc and mojeaudyty.pl numbers
- Section 3: Contact — simple CTA, no form needed at launch (email link is sufficient)
- Mobile responsive, no layout breakage at 375px
- Available in English and Polish with a language switcher
- Deployed to production via FTP

## Non-Goals for v1

- Blog
- Skills list or tech stack inventory
- Separate "About" page
- CV download
- Dark mode toggle
- CMS or admin interface
- Analytics (can be added post-launch)
- Contact form with backend (email link is sufficient)
- Case studies or long-form project writeups
